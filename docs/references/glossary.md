# Glosario — Mantenimiento Industrial / Farma

## KPIs
- **MTBF** — Mean Time Between Failures = Σ uptime / nº fallos.
- **MTTR** — Mean Time To Repair = Σ tiempo reparación / nº reparaciones.
- **MTTF** — Mean Time To Failure (no reparables).
- **Disponibilidad (A)** = Uptime / (Uptime + Downtime).
- **OEE** = Disponibilidad × Rendimiento × Calidad.
- **PM Compliance** = PM ejecutados a tiempo / PM planificados.
- **Backlog ratio** = horas backlog / capacidad semanal.

## Mantenimiento
- **PM** Preventive Maintenance · **PdM** Predictive Maintenance · **CM** Corrective.
- **RCM** Reliability Centered Maintenance.
- **TPM** Total Productive Maintenance (8 pilares).
- **FMEA / FMECA** Failure Mode and Effects (and Criticality) Analysis.
- **RCA** Root Cause Analysis.
- **CMMS / GMAO** Computerized Maintenance Management System.

## Lean
- **A3, PDCA, 5S, SMED, VSM, Kaizen, Gemba, OPL, JES, Ishikawa, 5 Why**.

## GMP / Farma
- **ALCOA+**: Attributable, Legible, Contemporaneous, Original, Accurate + Complete, Consistent, Enduring, Available.
- **CAPA** Corrective and Preventive Action.
- **IQ/OQ/PQ** Installation/Operational/Performance Qualification.
- **CPP** Critical Process Parameter · **CQA** Critical Quality Attribute.
- **OOS / OOT** Out Of Specification / Out Of Trend.
- **Change Control** · **Deviation** · **Audit Trail**.

## Seguridad
- **LOTO** Lockout-Tagout.
- **PTW** Permit To Work.
- **JSA / JHA** Job Safety / Hazard Analysis.
- **ATEX** Atmósferas explosivas (zonas 0/1/2 gas, 20/21/22 polvo).
- **ALARP** As Low As Reasonably Practicable.
